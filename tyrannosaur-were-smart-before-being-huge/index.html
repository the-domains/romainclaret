New Tyrannosaur Species Reveals How the King of the Dinosaurs Won Its Crown

Tyrannosaurus rex is an icon, a dinosaur known to nearly everyone on the planet. It doesn't get much more awesome than a 13-meter long, seven-ton superpredator that could bite through the bones of its prey. T. rex may be the undisputed king of the dinosaurs, but how did evolution produce such a marvellous creature, the biggest predator ever to live on land?
